## üìñ Introduction  
This project focuses on the design and development of a **multifunctional military ground robot** capable of performing surveillance, threat detection, camouflage adaptation, landmine identification, and basic medical support. The primary goal is to reduce risks to soldiers by automating hazardous tasks in combat zones, thereby enhancing operational efficiency and safety.

## üõ†Ô∏è Methodology  
1. **Surveillance & Facial Recognition**  
   - Captured live video feed using a camera.  
   - Processed images with OpenCV to identify intruders.  
   - Triggered automated defensive response on unknown face detection.  

2. **Landmine Detection & Disposal**  
   - Scanned terrain with metal sensors.  
   - Halted movement and activated digging mechanism for safe exposure.  

3. **Camouflage System**  
   - Analyzed surrounding RGB values.  
   - Adjusted RGB LEDs to blend robot with environment.  

4. **Medical Support**  
   - Dispensed first-aid kit via onboard mechanism.  
   - Simulated GPS-based hospital appointment booking.
